The Confident has two criteria for whom we will recommend:

  1. We know you personally (We have worked with you, worked on issues together on Drupal, met at conferences, etc) and/or
  2. We can validate your work from open source sources (blog posts, presentations, Drupal, Github)

If you meet those criteria, and are currently available for Drupal work, feel free to fill out this form.

If our company recommends you to someone, and they hire you, just let us know and we'll send them an invoice for our service.
